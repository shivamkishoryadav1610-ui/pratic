The Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and all the celestial objects that revolve around it. It includes planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

The Sun

The Sun is the center of the Solar System and is a massive ball of hot gases. It provides light and heat that make life on Earth possible.

Planets

There are eight planets in the Solar System including Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars. These planets revolve around the Sun in fixed paths called orbits.
